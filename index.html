Li Ding | 丁立

I'm a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UMass Amherst CICS, advised by Prof. Lee Spector. My research interests are evolutionary algorithms, deep learning, computer vision, autonomous vehicles, and quantum computing.

Previously, I was a Research Engineer at MIT, working on autonomous vehicles and human-centered AI with Dr. Lex Fridman and Dr. Bryan Reimer. I was also an ASP graduate student at MIT EECS, and TAed a few courses (including 6.S094). Prior to that, I was a Research Associate at the University of Rochester, working on video action recognition with Prof. Chenliang Xu.
