Research

My research focus is optimization algorithms for large models and AI agents, focusing on:

  • Open-Endedness: generative models and AI agents for open-ended tasks or environments.
  • Human-AI Alignment: preference-based learning, RLHF, and safe RL.

I'm also interested and have published in disciplines including Quantum ML and HCI. Before PhD, I worked on deep learning for autonomous driving, human cognition modeling, and action recognition.
