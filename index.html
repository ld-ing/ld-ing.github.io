Li Ding | 丁立

I'm a research engineer at MIT, working on computer vision and autonomous vehicles with Dr. Lex Fridman and Dr. Bryan Reimer. I'm also a part-time graduate student at MIT EECS. Prior to that, I got my Master's degree from the University of Rochester, working on video modeling with Prof. Chenliang Xu.

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning, and data science, especially on topics related to semi- and self-supervised learning with large-scale datasets. My current research focuses on driving scene understanding and human cognitive capability assessment. I also TAed a few courses at MIT, including 6.S094: Deep Learning for Self-Driving Cars.
