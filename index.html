Li Ding | 丁立

I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UMass Amherst CICS, advised by Prof. Lee Spector. My research interests are evolutionary machine learning, deep learning, genetic algorithms, and quantum computing.

Previously, I was a Research Engineer at MIT, working on computer vision, autonomous vehicles, and human-centered computing with Dr. Lex Fridman and Dr. Bryan Reimer. I was also a part-time graduate student at MIT EECS, and TAed a few courses (including 6.S094). Prior to that, I was a Research Associate at the University of Rochester, working on video action recognition with Prof. Chenliang Xu.
